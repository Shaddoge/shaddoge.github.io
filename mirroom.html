Mirroom

Project Description

Mirroom is an AR mobile puzzle game that requires image markers to place mirrors within the room to reflect the lasers being fired at a static location in order to hit and charge the pylon within the room. The player can get scores within the levels based on the time completed and mirrors placed.

This group project was made using C# and Unity with Vuforia package for our course: Development of Augmented or Virtual Reality Applications.
