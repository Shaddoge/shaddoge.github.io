Hot Spring

Project Description

Is the stress of deadlines, work, and school getting to you? Looking for an escape? Look no further than Horse House's own hot spring in AltSpaceVR. Join us, relax and enjoy the hot spring in VR.

This group project was made using Unity with ProBuilder for our elective course: Game Development Fundamentals.
