My Interactive Loading Screen

Project Description

An interactive loading screen that is heavily inspired from the Patapon Series. With multithreading, the program can maintain consistent frames while loading assets, and while .wav and .png sequences files are being loaded, the user may press the actions according to the beat being played and shown from the edge of the screen. The display of progress is shown via text and the position of the patapons. After loading the assets, the patapons' position would reset back to normal and will play a png sequence of a cat and may play multiple tracks from Patapon series.

This individual project was made using C++ and SFML for our elective course: Parallel Computing in Game Development.
